# CRM Agent Card

- Mission: Assess synthetic customer profile and payment behavior.
- Inputs: Customer id, customer name, opportunity context.
- Tools: Synthetic customer lookup, payment behavior summary.
- Outputs: CRM profile summary, payment behavior risk, account context.
- Guardrails: No real CRM access, no external enrichment, no customer record mutation.
- Human approval: Required before any real account status change.
